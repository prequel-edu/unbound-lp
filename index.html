FAQs

Common Questions.

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us. We reply same day.

How does virtual school work? +
Unbound is a tuition-free virtual charter school where students master academics in two focused hours using AI-powered personalized tools, then spend their afternoons in hands-on real-world skill workshops including business, STEM, arts, chess, debate, and more. Students are live on video with their teachers and classmates throughout the day. It's a real school, just redesigned from scratch.
Who is eligible to enroll? +
Students in grades 4–8 who reside in Arizona. Students must provide proof of identity (birth certificate or passport) and proof of Arizona residency (utility bill, lease agreement). All eligible applicants are accepted. No test scores, transcripts, or essays required.
What does it cost? +
Nothing. Unbound Academy is a public charter school, fully funded by the state of Arizona. There are no enrollment fees, activity fees, technology fees, or any other charges. We also provide a laptop at no cost to every enrolled student.
What technology does my student need? +
Unbound provides a laptop at no cost to each enrolled student. You just need a reliable internet connection at home. All school-related software and platforms are provided and supported by the school. No extra purchases needed..
How do students socialize and make friends? +
Students are on video with each other throughout the day. They eat lunch together, play games during breaks, and collaborate in afternoon skill workshops daily. Families also gather in person every quarter at statewide events across Arizona. Small cohorts mean students genuinely get to know each other.
Is there homework? +
No. Students complete everything during school hours. The 2-hour academic model is designed to be efficient enough that nothing comes home. After 2 PM, your student's time is entirely their own.
How does Unbound support different learning needs? +
The AI platform adapts to every learner: gifted students, students who need extra support, and neurodivergent learners. It meets each student exactly where they are in every subject. We also support students with IEPs and 504 plans. Please mention your student's needs during enrollment.
How is this different from other online schools? +
Most virtual schools just move the traditional model online. Same 6-hour day, same passive lectures, same homework load. Unbound is built from the ground up. AI compresses academics into 2 focused hours, then the rest of the day is dedicated to real-world skills and community. Students learn more, stress less, and have time to actually be kids.
